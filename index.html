About the Founder

Meet Chanu Bajaj

Chanu Bajaj founded Aashiyana with a simple but powerful mission: a world where animals are treated with the respect and care they deserve, free from cruelty and neglect. Alongside her team, she works tirelessly to rescue and treat animals in critical condition, provide them with food and shelter, and raise awareness through community education.

Her long-term dream is to build a sanctuary where rescued animals can live out their lives in peace and happiness, surrounded by the care they were once denied.

Our Vision

We Believe in a Better World for Souls in Need

We're working to build a place where animals can live happily and receive the best care possible. Our dream is a world free from cruelty, where animal rights are protected through advocacy, awareness, and consistent rescue efforts. Humans have the power to speak up for their own rights, but that same voice needs to be raised for the animals who can't speak for themselves. Caring means sharing that care.

Aashiyana means home. We rescue the most critical cases and treat many minor ones on the spot with the help of a paravet, taking every serious case to private clinics for full treatment. Once recovered, dogs are safely returned to their territory under the watch of a local caretaker, while aged or non-releasable animals stay permanently under our care. Feeding dogs, cats, and cows nutritious meals with supplements is part of our daily routine.

How We Help

Three pillars of our daily work for animals in need.

Animal Rescue

We go on quests to rescue and recover dogs and cats roaming our streets, responding to calls and reports of animals in distress.

Veterinary Services

We provide free veterinary treatment to strays, rescues, and pets, from basic checkups to complex surgical care.

Fostering & Adoption

We help our rescues find both temporary foster homes and forever families, giving every animal a chance at a better life.

From A Child's Heart to a Professional Sanctuary

What started as a childhood instinct to feed stray puppies has blossomed into a full-scale professional rescue operation. Aashiyana is not just a shelter; it is a promise that the most neglected animals will find medical recovery and lifelong love.

We specialize in the most critical cases—those that others might deem "unreachable." Our home is a place where pain is replaced by peace.

Specialized Recovery Units

Fighting the most fatal diseases with precision and care.

🦠

Parvovirus Isolation

Parvo is a death sentence for many puppies. We have created a specialized "Recovery Zone" to isolate infected puppies, giving them the intensive care and supplements needed to fight back and survive.

🩺

Critical Medical Care

From treating severe maggot infections and TVT tumors to rehabilitating paralyzed animals, our medical wing focuses on "Rescue Till Recovery."

Our Core Initiatives

Building a sustainable world where humans and animals coexist in harmony.

Sterilization

Preventing the birth of thousands of stray animals into a life of suffering. We have successfully spayed 400-500 animals to control the population.

Vaccinations

Our "Anti Rabies 9 in 1" vaccination drives protect whole communities from fatal diseases and ensure a healthier stray population.

Indie Adoption

We champion the beauty of "Indies." We advocate for the adoption of abandoned dogs and cats, giving them the forever homes they deserve.

The Wall of Miracles
